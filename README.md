Overview
This repository contains an executive-level sales performance dashboard for a fictional superstore dealing in Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology products. Key visualizations include:

Category and subcategory breakdowns by sales/profit.
Geographic heatmaps and top-state rankings.
Segment performance (Corporate, Consumer, Home Office).
Discount trends and their inverse effect on profitability.
Time-series forecasts for sales and profit through 2023.

The dashboard reveals strong growth in Technology (46.88% YoY) and urban markets like California, but highlights risks in high-discount Furniture sales and unprofitable segments like Texas/Home Office. Ideal for data visualization portfolios or business analytics demos
